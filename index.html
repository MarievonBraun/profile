Immediately after my bachelor's, I started a professional training course (similar to le Wagon but online) to become a User Interface Designer. While studying, I realized I would also like to be able to understand Web (and App) design & development from a developer's side. That decision brought me to Le Wagon and now allows me to complete my education to be a professional UI Designer.
